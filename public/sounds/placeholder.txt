Place your audio files here:

1. background-music.wav or background-music.mp3 - Looping background music
2. click-sound.wav or click-sound.mp3 - Sound effect for clicks

Both WAV and MP3 formats are supported. WAV files provide higher quality but are larger in size.

You can find free sound resources at:
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/music/
- Freesound: https://freesound.org/ 
- Mixkit: https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
